Rules and Guidelines

Participation Rules & Fair Play Guidelines

To ensure a smooth and fair competition, all participants must adhere to the following rules and guidelines. Failure to comply may result in disqualification.

Each team can have a maximum of 4 members.

All submissions must be original and developed during the hackathon.

Use of pre-existing code is allowed, but must be properly attributed.

All participants must join the official communication channels for updates.

Final submissions must be made before the deadline to be considered for evaluation.

How It Execute

3 Exciting Rounds

Round 1: Idea Submission

Submit a project proposal outlining your idea, problem statement, and proposed solution. Top innovative ideas advance to the next round.

Round 2: Prototype Development & Submission

Develop a functional prototype showcasing the core features and implementation of your idea. The best-performing teams move to the final round.

Round 3: Final Pitch

Present your project to judges, explaining its functionality, impact, and future potential. Winners will be announced based on evaluation criteria.
